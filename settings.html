About TaskFlow

TaskFlow v1.0.0

A modern, feature-rich task management application designed to help you stay productive and organized.

Features

  • Project Management
  • Task Tracking
  • Calendar View
  • Pomodoro Timer
  • Data Analytics
  • Local Data Storage

Technology

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All your data is stored locally in your browser.

Browser Support

Works best on modern browsers that support ES6 and localStorage APIs.
